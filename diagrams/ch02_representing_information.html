See how addition overflows in unsigned and two's complement 4-bit arithmetic.

Key Insight

In w-bit unsigned arithmetic, if a+b ≥ 2w, the result wraps around (mod 2w). In two's complement, positive overflow produces a negative result, and the sum of two negatives can produce a positive result.
